The MecWarriors

Sep 2013 - Jun 2015

About

The MecWarriors team was founded to share our knowledge of Unity’s Mecanim animation system with developers struggling to adopt the new technology in their projects. At one point, we even acted as peer reviewers of a published Mecanim book! We regularly posted new tutorials on our blog site, and as freelancers we worked with clients around the world on their game projects. We mostly performed Mecanim-related tasks, but we supported a range of other development needs as well.
