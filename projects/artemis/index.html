Artemis

May 2013
Game Design Student at Columbia College Chicago

About

Artemis was an installation project designed with Kinect motion controls in mind, and it was part of a live exhibit at Columbia College Chicago. People would walk in, wave at the Kinect camera, and then use their hands (and sometimes their whole bodies!) to lead a flying whale around a space environment. It was fun to make and very fun to watch.
